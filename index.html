Message From The Principal

Euro Kids has been involved in the growth of kids from an early age with their preschools. At Euro Kids, we work towards exploring the innate potential of each child, believing each one to be unique. Our goal is to guide them to explore, help them to acquire skills and instill in them the joy of learning, which would set the tone for the rest of their lives.

One of the most important benefits of coming to Euro Kids is the social interaction and learning to be self-sufficient. The children here are safe – physically and emotionally. Young children learn to follow a routine and to get along with people who are not their relatives. Interacting with other children means learning how to wait, how to take turns and how to listen. They learn social skills when they interact with other children. These social skills are critical to a child’s developing personality.

Euro Kids also engages children in a wide spectrum of age-appropriate learning experiences, such as music and movement, art and craft, dramatic play, sensory play and storytelling.

‘PLAYING, LEARNING AND ENJOYING EVERY MINUTE’ is what Euro Kids is all about.

Shailaja Adhikary
Principal, EuroKids Nepal
